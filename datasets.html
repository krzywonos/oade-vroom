DATASETS

This page contains an overview of every dataset which is used in this project. The datasets are related to two countries and are divided into two categories. The one related to air quality index and the others related to public and private transportation.

Original Datasets

Ds1: Centraline qualità dell'aria - Bologna

Air quality data from 2017 to 2024, relating to the three monitoring stations located in the municipality of Bologna. The data were processed starting from those made available by ARPAE Emilia Romagna, which can be consulted at the following address.

The stations are the following:

  1. Giardini Margherita
  2. Via Chiarini
  3. Porta San Felice

Ds2: parco circolante veicoli - Bologna

Circulating fleet of the Municipality of Bologna divided by postal code of residence of the owner.

    For each postal code the quantities are indicated for:
  • Reference year
  • Fuel
  • Owner age
  • Euro standard
  • Owner/company gender
  • Vehicle age

The data are the result of an agreement between the Municipality of Bologna and ACI whose complete data are available on the pages

Ds6: Low emission buses used for local public transport - Milano

Low-emission buses used for local public transport - indicator per 100 buses and absolute value - Historical series

The dataset shows, starting from 2014, the low-emission buses used for local public transport in the Municipality of Milan (indicator per 100 buses used and absolute value).

Electric buses (hybrid or all-electric traction, including those powered by hydrogen with fuel cell technology) and gas-powered buses (with bi-fuel petrol/methane or petrol/LPG engine) are considered low-emission.

Dataset extrapolated from tables 7.1-7.2 Urban Mobility Istat.
