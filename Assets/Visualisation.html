Milano vehicle types

The chart shows the total amount of vehicle types for each cathegories for the years 2017-2024.
For readability purposes the column of the dataset have been summarized as follow:

  • MERCI: autocarri trasporto merci, motocarri e quadricicli trasporto merci, rimorchi e semirimorchi trasporto merci
  • SPECIALI: autoveicoli speciali specifici, motoveicoli e quadricicli speciali specifici, rimorchi e semirimorchi speciali
